# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Palestinian owners and decision-makers at small and medium businesses who need practical help organizing operations, resolving urgent business challenges, and growing sustainably. They visit the website to quickly understand what Sobol offers and request a consultation.

## Product Purpose

Sobol presents a credible local business-development partner that provides administrative and marketing consulting grounded in the Palestinian market. The website should communicate the offer quickly, build confidence, and lead qualified visitors toward requesting a consultation.

## Brand Personality

Calm, credible, and practical. The voice should feel experienced and reassuring without exaggeration, visual noise, or unnecessary claims.

## Anti-references

Avoid long and distracting landing pages, dark or visually heavy hero sections, unsupported performance statistics, decorative metric dashboards, unrelated floating icons, excessive cards, and continuous attention-seeking animation.

## Design Principles

1. Communicate the value proposition within one focused viewport.
2. Earn trust through clarity and relevance rather than promotional statistics.
3. Show a practical path from business complexity to organized growth.
4. Give the consultation action clear priority.
5. Respect Arabic reading flow and the local Palestinian business context.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction standards. Preserve clear Arabic RTL reading order, visible keyboard focus, semantic structure, and reduced-motion preferences. Do not rely on color alone to communicate meaning.
